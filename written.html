About This Collection

These writings are moments captured in time—some remain unchanged, some are revisited, but all carry the truth of where they began.

Soft Whispers

(original — Sept 2025)

you listen to me with intention
make me feel pretty without trying
wanted in a way I didn’t ask for
but somehow needed

every time I look at you
I forget how to be anything but soft

(revisited — Jan 2026)

you listen to me with intention
make me feel seen without asking

I forget how to be guarded
when you’re near

written in a moment of softness I didn’t question

Terrified

(original — March 2025)

terrified..
that’s how I felt
when I almost lost you

everything went quiet
except my mind

(revisited — Feb 2026)

terrified

I couldn’t breathe
couldn’t think
couldn’t speak

everything inside me
collapsed at once

I didn’t change the feeling—just how it was carried
