Simply Seen

A curated space of poems that were shared, noticed, and remembered. These are voices that reached beyond the page.

Silent Storm

I carry thunder in quiet bones,
rain that never finds the ground.
The sky inside me never sleeps.

Paper Skies

I folded dreams into paper skies,
watched them drift where silence lies.
Every crease a memory.

Unsaid Things

There are words I never spoke,
still echoing in empty rooms.
They know my name too well.

City Echoes

Voices bounce between stone walls,
the city remembers everything
even what we forget.
